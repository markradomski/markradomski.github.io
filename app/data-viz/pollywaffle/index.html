Smashed Avocado on Toast Property Price Index

Australian Federal Politician property ownership

Inspired by demographer Bernard Salt triggering The Smashed Avocado War amongst Millennials, this visualisation has mapped out how many years of breakfasts you would have to give up to afford the same number of properties as various Politicians.
(based on a median house price of $656,800AUD and eating $22 avo on toast every.single.day)
