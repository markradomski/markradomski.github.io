There will be blood

Timelapse of Australia's unemployment rate from 2010 - 17

Wanting to try out the R language, this heat map is based on Nathan Yau's great animated map of US unemployment.
(Un)fortunately, Australia missed most of the GFC - partly due to the mining boom, so the blood letting isn't nearly as impressive/terrifying as the US.
