Preppr android app

React Native proof of concept

There was this one time I bumped into a Russian iOS developer at a co-working space in Thailand (stay with me), and she was working on this Instagram post scheduler thing called Preppr. Turns out there are millions of people who post multiple times a day - some even do it multiple times per hour! And of course, they don't have an android version. Having never used React Native before, I wondered (out loud) if I could clone the iOS version.

So this is the end result. If it looks like I knocked this together at a weekend hackthon - you'd be right.
